ParticleMap.js

Construct delightful, particle-backed, maps with ease.

ParticleMap.js is a simple, lightweight, depedency-free javascript library for generating point filled maps from GeoJSON files. Writes to canvas (it's fast) and has an easy to use API. Weighs in at less than XKB minified.
Flexible API let's you do cool things like a fancy pulsing circle effect over your current city. Just like the movies!
Or give a special shout out to a special country in your life. It's all super easy to do.
Reads and parses and valid GeoJSON file. Toss your backyard or a nation into it - you'll have particle delicousnes in no time.
